ABOUT ME

I am a software developer from New Delhi, India. I started my career as a software developer in October 2015, with the vision to provide Excellence in software solution.

As a software developer I bring innovative ideas and cutting edge technologies into business of customers. I specialise in providing high technology, end-to-end solutions in mobile apps and web development with Angular JS/Ionic with the experience of 10+ Hybrid Apps and E-com portals at corporate level.

I ensure you of quality services, fully responsiveness, and tested in a wide range of devices. I consider design to be just as important as development and aim to combine both to produce high quality user experience.

I take great care to ensure each project is well-documented and easily maintainable so you can upgrade the software as your business grows. I have been working professionally in software development and dabbled into Digital Marketing, Social Media, Teaching, Gaming and Blogging.
